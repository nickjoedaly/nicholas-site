Play modes
Multiple rulesets for different creative needs
Generative
The Spread
Lay out cards in positions that govern melody, harmony, rhythm, form, and character. Each position constrains one dimension of the piece you'll write.
Position 1: Melody
Position 2: Harmony
Position 3: Rhythm
Position 4: Form
Position 5: Character / wild
Workshop
The Swap
Bring an existing piece to the table. Draw cards to challenge specific aspects — swap a card to replace a technique, layer one to add a new dimension, discard to simplify.
Draw 3 cards. Choose one to apply.
Discard one element of your piece.
Replace it with the card's technique.
Collaborative
The Round
Multiple composers draw from the same deck, building a collective set of constraints. Each player adds one card to a shared spread, negotiating the emerging piece together.
Each player draws 2 cards.
Take turns adding to the spread.
Discuss, negotiate, compose.
Reflective
The Browse
No game mechanics — simply read through the deck as a compendium of techniques. Each card is a mini-essay on a compositional possibility, with references to the repertoire.
Read one card per day.
Consider how it applies to your
current work or thinking.
