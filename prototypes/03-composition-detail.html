Program note

Meridian Lines traces the invisible boundaries that divide and connect — time zones, political borders, the liminal space between waking and sleep. Each of the three movements explores a different kind of threshold, using the string quartet as an acoustic body that the electronics gradually infiltrate and extend.

The opening movement establishes a slow-breathing harmonic field rooted in Phrygian modality, over which spectral processing reveals hidden overtones. The second movement fractures this stillness into rapid gestural exchanges, while the finale attempts a reconciliation between the organic and the synthetic.
